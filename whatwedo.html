who we are
nefilim is a company dedicated to combating human trafficking through the use of osint (open-source intelligence). our primary mission is to employ advanced techniques for information collection and analysis to identify and confront human trafficking networks on a global scale. additionally, nefilim is committed to sharing its expertise in osint by providing training and educational resources to empower other organizations and individuals to contribute to the fight against this heinous crime. we are determined to make a positive impact by raising awareness and providing effective tools for those who wish to actively engage in the eradication of human trafficking.
our team
our team is led by ceo pedro avi, he is certified by mcafee, aws, comptia, and has experience with the brazilian federal police, interpol, and europol. the other analysts prefer to remain undisclosed for security reasons, but the team is composed of various professionals from around the world.
